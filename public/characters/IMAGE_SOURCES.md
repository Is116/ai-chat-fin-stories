## Quick Download Instructions

1. Visit the suggested URLs above
2. Select high-quality images (minimum 800x800px recommended)
3. Download and rename them according to the filenames above
4. Place them in the `public/characters/` directory

## AI-Generated Alternative

If you prefer AI-generated images, you can use:
- **DALL-E**: https://openai.com/dall-e
- **Midjourney**: https://midjourney.com
- **Stable Diffusion**: Various implementations available

Use prompts like: "Portrait of [character name] from [book], [era/style], realistic, high quality"

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 800x800px minimum (square format recommended)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 or 4:3 preferred
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB for optimal loading

## License Considerations

Make sure to:
- Use only images with appropriate licenses for your use case
- Credit photographers if required by the license
- Use public domain or Creative Commons images when possible
